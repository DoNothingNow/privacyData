This is an application to manage your own hotel check-in records. For business people who go on business trips for a long time, you can use this product to record your daily trip (staying in the hotel), meeting minutes, feelings and so on. So that you can query your past records later. This product is an independent application that does not need to be connected to the Internet. All data will only be recorded locally and will not be uploaded to any server. We respect the privacy of all customers and will not store any content you record in the application.
