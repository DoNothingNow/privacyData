This is an application that manages your own travel records. For long-term business people, you can use this product to record your daily trip (stay in the hotel), meeting minutes, feelings and so on. So that you can query past records later.

The product is a standalone application that does not require a connection to the Internet. All data will be recorded locally and will not be uploaded to any server. We respect the privacy of all our customers and will not store any of your records in our applications.
